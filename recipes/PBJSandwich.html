PB&J Sandwich

Description

A very simple but beloved sandwich that is easy to make and very satisfying

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread
  • Peanut butter (Crunchy or smooth)
  • Jelly (Grape is best)

Steps

  1. Take two pieces of bread
  2. On one of them, spread peanut butter
  3. On the second piece of bread, spread jelly
  4. Bring pieces of bread together, on the ingredients size
